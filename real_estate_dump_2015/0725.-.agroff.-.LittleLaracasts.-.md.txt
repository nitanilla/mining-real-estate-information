Little Laracasts
===============

Adds a button underneath videos on laracasts.com to let you toggle between an expanded or non-expanded view.
The expanded view will use the entirety of your browser window to show the video, allowing for a more compact use of screen real-estate when desired.

You can install it here:

https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/little-laracasts/geedjamdkhiejjpablegmfbghnoahkoh
