Funda Pro
=========

[Chrome Extension](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/funda-pro/coemhglclonojcfhmogfklfhpmfhafei) that extends interaction experience with Dutch online real estate base [Funda.nl](http://www.funda.nl).

If you have any feature request or want to submit a bug [use this section please](https://github.com/uoziod/chrome-funda-pro/issues).
