# WPCasa Ninja Forms #
[![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/license-GPL%202.0%2B-lightgrey.svg?style=flat-square)](https://github.com/wpsight/wpcasa/blob/master/wpcasa/LICENSE)

The WPCasa Ninja Forms add-on is a bridge plugin for the Ninja Forms form builder that can be used to display a contact form on single listing pages. The add-on makes sure that useful property information is attaced to the emails sent through the form. It also comes with a starter form that includes all the necessary fields to make your life easier. Please find more information at [wpcasa.com](https://wpcasa.com/downloads/wpcasa-ninja-forms/).

**Please Notice!** This is an extension plugin and only works in combination with the [WPCasa Real Estate WordPress](https://github.com/wpsight/wpcasa) plugin.
