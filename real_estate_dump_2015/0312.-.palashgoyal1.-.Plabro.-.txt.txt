JSON file involves chat of real estate brokers. The .R code involves:
1. Data cleaning and creating corpus.
2. Labeling a collection of words occurring together.
3. Stemming n removing nos.
4. Making DTM and putting a constraint on the weightages of 'rent' and 'sell' words with other words
5. Doing a hierarchiacal clustering and clustering words on basis of similarity
6. Term Correlations : Finding Association using 'corlimit' and 'findAssocs'
