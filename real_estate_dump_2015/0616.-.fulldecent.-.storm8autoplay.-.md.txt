Summary
==========

This games automates the leveling-up steps of Storm8 games. Fully automated play is available for most games.

Cheating is not directly supported for Mobsters, Rockstar, Zombies Live, Ninjas Live, Vampires Live, World War, and whatever other new ones they come out with, but these clients will do MOST of the work for you.


Functionality
=============

 * Automatically find and trade codes to join armies
 * Perform financial analysis of real estate options and purchase options to maximize compound return on investment
 * Run missions
 * Attack other players


Usage
=====

Find your own player ID by playing the game while connected to WireShark or Charles.app

Edit the file to use your own player ID (cookie).

On Mac or Linux, run each of these scripts in your cron job or from the command line.

Submit improvements back here.
