NYCPolyline
===========

Adds a polyline overlay of Manhattan neighborhoods with geojson.  [Reference Google Developer Docs](https://developers.google.com/maps/documentation/javascript/datalayer)

Great for real estate sites.  Coordinate point json credit to: [PediaCities](http://nycdata.pediacities.com/dataset/pediacities-nyc-neighborhoods/resource/0f97a739-0a9f-442f-8105-43164d3af6f8)
