This plugin enables easy embedding of NabeWise neighborhood widgets into your wordpress blog

=== Description ===

With this plugin, you can add NabeWise widgets to any post on your blog.

=== Installation ===

1. Upload `nabewise.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Get an API key from http://nabewise.com/widgets
4. In your wordpress admin, click on NabeWise settings and put in your API key
   and your display options in the form
5. Place `<?php do_action('nabewise_widget'); ?>` in your template where you want the
   widget to display.
