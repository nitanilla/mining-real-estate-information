== proprietas

Build status: {<img src="https://secure.travis-ci.org/ajokela/proprietas.png" />}[http://travis-ci.org/ajokela/proprietas]

The general idea of this project is provide a way to slice and dice GIS data (e.g. maps) along with other sources of geolocated data.
Initially, this will be real estate data for Ramsey County, MN.  As other things pique my interest, they will be added. 

See doc/notes.txt for more information.
