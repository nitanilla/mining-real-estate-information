== README

RealEstateWeb

http://realestateweb.herokuapp.com/

Is a free App for Real Estate operations. An user can sign up and sell/rent or just look for a department or house located at the place and with the conditions the user is looking for.

* Ruby version: 
  Ruby 2.1.2p95
  Rails 4.2.0

* System dependencies
  MySQL 5.1
