# Pro-forma
A real estate mobile property evaluation application for android and ios.
			ABOUT
*********************************************************
	This is a real-estate pro-forma evaluation application that aids indivifuals or firms interested in real estate properties to go through the datails of a property before settling on purchase. It is aimed to make the process less tiresome and the documents easily accessible anywhere and anytime.
