# CryptoWorld


https://cwv.io/



CryptoWorld
Blockchain Build The World
An entry-level gaming scenarized entrance of Blockchain world
CWV public chain is the world's first integrated main chain based on multi chains. It is a real Blockchain 3.0 product. CryptoWorld.vip is the first entry-level application platform based on CWV created by an 1:1 mapping to the real world. It is a scenarized virtual world linking various Blockchains and technologies.







CryptoWorld.Vip
A Cryptographic World
CryptoWorld.vip synthesizes various cryptographic digital assets from all over the world. It aggregates a large number of issued digital currencies of Blockchain services and provides them a usage and circulation scenario. CryptoWorld is a construction space of virtual estates on the virtual Earth. Different estate space has its own functional properties making it connect to different Blockchain services.
