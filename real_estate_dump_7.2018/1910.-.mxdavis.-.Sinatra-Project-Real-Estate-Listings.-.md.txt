# Sinatra-Project-Real-Estate-Listings

Welcome to our Real Estate Listings Site

Create yourself a user, view all listings, and add, edit, and delete your listings.

Fork and clone the repo to your computer.

Run bundle install.

Run shotgun if running locally, and view in your browser at http://127.0.0.1:9393/

For more information on why and how this app was created please visit http://malki.rocks/my-first-web-app-from-scratch/
