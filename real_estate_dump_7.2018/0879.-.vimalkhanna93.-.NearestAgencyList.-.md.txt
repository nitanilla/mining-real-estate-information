NearestAgencyList
=================

This application gives you the list of real estate agencies within 10 miles radius of the given address. 

Technical aspects of the application:
-------------------------------------
- Javascript and JQuery along with HTML is used to develop the application.
- Data about real estate agencies and places are fetched from Google Places API.
- Geo-location co-ordinates of the listed address is fetched and the real estate agencies within 10 miles radius of it is displayed
