[Product Overview: Crypto currency and fiat 100% are involved in the construction/flipping of real estate. After the sale of a real estate - tokens are redeemed at the selling price of the property or tokens give the right to receive rental income from real estate object.
It’s a tool for investors and developers. Focused on investors with any size of capital (privates and legal entities) on the one hand and developers (entrepreneurs, companies and investors), working with real estate on the other side. Platform eliminates intermediaries and reduces costs for capital transfer, giving protection and security of tenure shares. Investors are free to decide whom to trust their money and how much to trust. Developers use investors’ capital quickly, without intermediaries, without any overpayments. Global target of platform - integration with national cadastral records all over the world except the United States and facilitation of rights register.]
