BlueSpruce Network is the [Amazon Mechanical Turk](https://www.mturk.com/) of real estate due diligence.

Instead of compensating Turkers with nickels and dimes, we compensate them with equity in commercial real estate. Their ownership is written to the Ethereum block chain as a smart contract, and that contract is legally enforceable via offchain legal structures.
