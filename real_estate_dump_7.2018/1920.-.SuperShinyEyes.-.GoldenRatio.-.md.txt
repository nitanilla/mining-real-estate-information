# GoldenRatio for Aalto Data Science Hackathon 2015
Golden Ratio is a smart real estate web app. It enables you to search apartments in relation to your school or work places. We used open HSL data and some private real estate data. Please check the demo video:

[![Youtube video](/readme_images/youtube_thumbnail.png)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=p9yMRqvzQqw)
