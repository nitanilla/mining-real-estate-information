# EmlaX
EmlaX is a real estate portfolio program.

Database Warning!

Before using this program, you must setup mysql database in your computer and integrate your mysql information(username-password) to program.if you dont do this,it will not work well.

What can you do in EmlaX?

You can add new homes and land in your portfolio.

You can update and delete your real estate's properties(price,owner phone,mail...).

You can search optimum home or land for your customer.

You can let them know via email inside program with several subjects.

But Do not forget to setup mysql on your computer.
