Cool Companies on Github
===================


Overview
-------------

I couldn't find a listing or a way to search for cool big companies that are on github so I made one. The categories are in no particular order nor are the companies listed in the category in any order. Category names stand to be improved, some of the companies may fall under many categories but I didn't want the list to have dupes.

See any companies missing? Have a better way to categorize? Please make a PR :D
