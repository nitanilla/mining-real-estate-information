== Description ==
This plugin installs a customizable  [Jumbo Mortgage Rates Widget](http://www.freerateupdate.com/mortgage-rate-widget "Jumbo Mortgage Rates Widget") that you can drag into your sidebar in the widget area of wordpress. The Jumbo mortgage rates widget is updated daily by FreeRateUpdate.com who researches wholesale mortgage lender's rate sheets and updates their  [Jumbo mortgage rates](http://www.freerateupdate.com/ "Jumbo mortgage rates") multiple times per day.



== Installation ==



1. Upload plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= How do I customize widget's look and feel? =

Just add the widget to your sidebar, and you'll be able to customize it in the wigdet settings.
