# Parallel-Ledger
Real Estate Transaction Solution 
 
Parallel Ledger provides a secure blockchain software solution for real estate transactions. It allows simple management of Vacant Land, Buildings,Commercial and Residential property and tracking of addresses, roads and infrastructure for transparent data discovery. Our platform enables Smart contracts for purchasing, selling or verifying all real estate assets including titles and mortgage deeds, liens and leases. Each user whether Lawyer, banker or RE Broker has secured ID access rights and permissions to certain search and transaction criteria related to their role in the transaction.
