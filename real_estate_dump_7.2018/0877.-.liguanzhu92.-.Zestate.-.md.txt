# Zestate
House rental and real estate selling application pattern.  
## Features  
* Designed for Buyer and Seller, which navigate to different interface.  
* Search property by zip, area name and display conformed properties in maps or list  
* Make appointment with vender directly  
* Push notification when new property added to the web service  
* Taking photo directly or upload image through the device for your new property.  
* Save your property draft in local  
* Check your appointment easily and add to your device calendar  

## Android Version  
5.0 or up
