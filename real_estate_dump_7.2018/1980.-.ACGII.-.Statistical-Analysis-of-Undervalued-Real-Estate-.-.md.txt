# Statistical-Analysis-of-Undervalued-Real-Estate

Coursera Statistics with R Project

Duke University

Real Estate Data for houses in Ames,Iowa, is evaluated. A predictive model is developed and used to evaluate both undervalued and overvalued house prices. The evaluation is perfromed using training, test and validation data that is provided. The results are presented and explained.
