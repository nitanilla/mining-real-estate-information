<h1>
    <strong>AFTLab Affiliates</strong>
    <strong></strong>
</h1>
<p>
    <strong>Affiliate Membership</strong>
</p>
<em>
        We are grateful for the generous support of our 2017 Affiliate members:
        State Street Global and BBVA.
    </em>
<p>
    $175,000 per year membership supports the students, research and
    educational goals of the AFTLab.
</p>
<p>
    <strong>Affiliate Membership Benefits</strong>
</p>
<p>
    · Invitation to all AFTLab events
</p>
<p>
    · Named sponsorship and table at Annual AFTLab Fintech Forum
</p>
<p>
    · Company may send one Visiting Fellow to work on-campus alongside the
    AFTLab
</p>
<p>
    · One seat on the AFTLab Advisory Board
</p>
<p>
    · Hosted executive visits to Stanford campus
</p>
<p>
    · Student recruiting opportunities
</p>
<p>
    · Real time advisory of research agenda
</p>

       This program is governed by Stanford University’s published policies on research and treatment of research results. <p>
    <strong>Annual Retreat</strong>
</p>
<p>
    · Intimate, senior-level retreat with AFTLab Faculty &amp; Advisory Board
</p>
<p>
    · Strategic Discussion &amp; Lab Update: Goals and Direction, State of the
    Union, Panel Discussion
</p>
<p>
    · Networking Dinner
</p>
<p>
    <strong>Special Workshops</strong>
</p>
<p>
    · 3-hour afternoon workshop format
</p>
<p>
    · Hands on modules taught by faculty, industry leaders, senior PhD
</p>
<p>
    · Rotating topics may include:
</p>
<p>
    Deep Learning
</p>
<p>
    NLP for Financial Data
</p>
<p>
    Fraud Detection
</p>
<p>
    <strong></strong>
</p>
<p>
    <strong>PhD Student Research Talks</strong>
</p>
<p>
    · 5-8 talks per quarter
</p>
<p>
    · Doctoral students discuss their research and solicit real-time feedback
</p>
<p>
    · Opportunity to give input in how research could be framed
</p>
<p>
    <strong></strong>
</p>
<p>

</p>
<p>
    · Once a week speakers (8 total) in Seminar sequence per quarter
</p>
<p>
    · Autumn quarter (Industry focused)
</p>
<p>
    · Spring quarter (Research focused)
</p>
<p>
    · Last session features top posters from AFTLab project courses
