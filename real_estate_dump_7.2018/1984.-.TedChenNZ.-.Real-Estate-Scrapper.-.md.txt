# Real-Estate-Scrapper

Scrapes some simple data off websites for real estate rentals and converted into TSV (tab seperated values) to be easily inputted into a spreadsheet.

Currently supported:
  * trademe.co.nz
  * realestate.co.nz
  
Usage:

  `python3 scrapper.py http://trademe.co.nz/urlstuffhere`
  
or

  `python3 scrapper.py input.txt output.txt`
